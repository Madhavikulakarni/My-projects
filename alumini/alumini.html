About Sharnbasva University

Sharnbasva University, located in Kalaburagi, Karnataka, is a pioneering institution dedicated to holistic education and values-based learning. Established under the vision of the Sharnbasveshwar Vidya Vardhak Sangha. Sharnbasva University offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across disciplines such as engineering, science, commerce, arts, and humanities.

The university campus is equipped with modern infrastructure, state-of-the-art laboratories, digital classrooms, and an extensive library to foster a dynamic learning environment. Beyond academics, Sharnbasva University encourages students to engage in extracurricular activities, leadership training, and social service. Guided by the principles of Basava Philosophy, the institution emphasizes integrity, equality, and the pursuit of knowledge for societal upliftment.
